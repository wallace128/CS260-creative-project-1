Classics

The Great Gatsby

Is wealth good or bad? What is love? Is love enough? Explore these questions of the soul in reading this amazing book.

The Lord of the Flies

What will happen if boys are left to govern themselves on a deserted island? What is truly inside of man? This book changed my life with these questions.

Crime and Punishment

This book explores with the reader whether bad actions are ever justified in a suspense filled cat and mouse style book.

The Adventures of Tom Sawyer

The mischief of a young boy cannot be outmatched! I loved reading the stories of Tom as a young boy.
