Nathan Wallace's Book Suggestions

I am an avid reader! I have read fantasy books, classics, sci-fi, novels, history books, historical fiction and other genres. Throughout my life, I have sought out the book recommendations of family, friends and internet strangers. Despite seeking out all of this advice, I still struggled to consistently find books that intrigued me. To fix this problem, I am creating this website as a local place for me to share my own recommendations with others and to receive recommendations from you! A lot of people use Paypal, or Penpals, but I would love to have some Bookpals!


Please send me a book suggestion through this text box and let's start talking!
